Encore!Encore!

I'm a philosopher. What they don't always tell you about philosophers is that we are well-trained in logic. Some of us are even well trained in symbolic logic- you know, the if A=C and B=C then A=B kind of stuff. We like to sit around and do proofs most of the day. An even smaller group, of which I was a part, actually study metalogic, which is cool. (My metalogic professor had a sidegig at IBM working on AI back in the 90's.) Shocking I know, but there are very few jobs for philosophers. I mean, unless you are Greek and can time travel back to Rome.

Unsurprisingly, there are lots of jobs for MBAs in Finance and Economics. Unfortunately for me, most of those jobs are either boring or require a lot of time spend commuting. But I did them. For 15 years I worked at a giant multinational company. It required long hours, boring meetings and relocating 4 times (BNA-HKG-CDG-HND-BNA) in a 6-year period to grow my career. Until it didn't.

When the pandemic hit and working from home became the norm, somehow hours got longer and the demands even greater. I burned out, soon joining others in the search for something more meaningful. That leads me here to software development.

So here I am for my second act returning again to logic. Looking to leverage those skills to contribute and create value during my encore!
